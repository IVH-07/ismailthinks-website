01 Philosophy

"Process should serve people. Not the other way around."

I've spent the better part of a decade inside the translation layer — the space where strategy meets execution, where ideas either gain traction or quietly die. What I've learned is that most failure isn't a talent problem or a strategy problem. It's a systems problem. The wrong process slows the right people down.

My approach is rooted in human behaviour and genuine engagement. Delivering authentic value to people — and bringing them along in the process of delivering it — is both the method and the goal. Data and AI are powerful tools, but only in service of that human intent.
